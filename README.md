## THE LAST MESSAGE

### PLOT

A bloody war between humanity and alien monsters has been raging for several decades. A human cruiser has been ambushed and is on the verge of being overrun by the monsters. By a twist of fate, an ordinary surgeon — played by the player — must fight his way through hordes of enemies and deliver a message that could turn the tide of the war in humanity’s favor.

>*The game is a 2D platformer set in a science-fiction universe with horror elements. The player navigates linear levels, defeats monsters, and collects supplies.*

### LAUNCHING

To start the story, go to https://github.com/cillyt/The-Last-Message/releases/tag/release and download The.Last.Message.zip, then extract the archive to a location of your choice. To launch the game, open The.Last.Message.exe. Do not run the game outside of its folder, as this may cause unpredictable crashes.
